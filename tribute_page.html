Who is Linus Torvalds?

Linus Torvalds is a software engineer and is the creator and one of the main developers of the Linux kernel, used by Linux Distributions and other operating systems such as Android and Chrome OS. He was born on December the 28th, 1969, in Helsinki, Finland.
